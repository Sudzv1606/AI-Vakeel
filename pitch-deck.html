Multi-Agent Swarm Architecture

01 — The Problem

Legal access in India
is broken.

₹5K–₹20K+

Lawyers are expensive. Just to draft a complaint — putting justice out of reach for millions of Indians.

90%+

Never file a complaint. Citizens don't know which law applies, which forum to approach, or how to cite sections.

Weeks

The process is slow. Finding the right sections, drafting in proper format, and getting review takes days or weeks.

40M+

Pending cases in Indian courts. A massive gap between legal need and legal access for 1.4 billion people.
